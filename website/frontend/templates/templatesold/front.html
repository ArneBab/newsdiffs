Tracking Changes in Online News Articles Over Time

NewsDiffs allows you to compare evolving versions of online news articles as they change over time, starting with The New York Times.

Sometimes the changes are minor, small edits in language or correction of spelling mistakes. Other times, the stories change and evolve rapidly, as a result of breaking news. It is also interesting to see the lead and substance of the article as it changes.

The records begin on June 17, 2012, as the project was inspired by the Knight Mozilla MIT Hackathon over that weekend.
